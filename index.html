Our story

It didn't start with a plan.
It started with a wrong animal.

August
15
It was just a random day, the 15th of August. I decided to start learning German because I was heading to Germany, and there, on Tandem, I saw your profile picture—an alpaca, unbothered and magnificent. I asked if it was a llama. You knew it wasn't, but you answered me anyway.

The funniest part is that you didn't even want to go to that park that day, but you went. Somehow, the universe nudged you there just so I could find you. We spent that first day talking about war and history; right then, I realized how smart and captivating you are, but I still hadn't understood how much I was going to love you.
October
Germany
In October, my new adventure began. I was almost alone, but I still had someone — and that someone was you, liebe. You were in Russia. Thousands of kilometres apart — and somehow that distance made everything clearer. We talked. We laughed. We stayed up too late. I even got a little jealous, like an Ebobo. We were already feeling it. We just didn't have the courage to say it yet.
New
Year's
You hate New Year's. I know. But that night, at the exact same moment, without any coordination, we both wrote long messages. Sent them simultaneously — confessions we'd been carrying for months. I think about that often. How we both reached for our phones, heart pounding, and wrote the same truth at the same time.
February
The month of lovers... haha.

In all seriousness, this month was very hard for us. I know that most of it was my fault — I created problems where there were none, and we ended up arguing. But once again, thanks to you, I understood that I was going down the wrong path.

Thank you so much, amore, for your patience and for helping me see things clearly. Even in the hardest moments, you teach me how to be a better man.
March
We talked with our hearts wide open, finally saying everything we felt and showing just how much we love each other. It was a month where we felt as much Ebobo as the love we carry — and we both agreed on that.

There were no more filters, no more doubts. Just the two of us, wanting to live together on Ebobo Island, choosing each other completely, and realizing that what we have is rarer than anything else in this world.

Italy and Russia. Two countries, two languages, one app where none of this was supposed to happen. You came to revise Italian. I came to learn German. We found something neither of us was looking for — and somehow, that made it even more ours. The distance is real. But so is every word we've ever exchanged. And that is so much larger than any map.

From that first alpaca photo to this moment, you’ve changed my world. And I never want to change it back.
